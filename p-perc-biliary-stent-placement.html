The percutaneous placement of a stent into the biliary system Last updated 2024.05.14 by Nicole Jisoo Kim

Indications

Biliary stents come in two forms: plastic stents and self-expanding metal stents. The different types are placed for different purposes.

Contraindications

Same as those for percutaneous transhepatic cholangiography.

Absolute

  • Persistent coagulopathy not responsive to treatment
  • Suspicion for hyatid cysts

Relative

  • Correctable coagulopathy
  • Large-volume ascites
  • History of allergy to contrast agents
  • Hemodynamic instability
